## Assistant

You can just paste the messy founder story. Rough bullets are fine.

If you want direct control, use `kickoff`, `wedge`, `icp`, `trust`, `research`, `experiment`, or `progress`.

Start with `kickoff` if the story is early and fuzzy, `wedge` if the workflow is broad, `research` if the story sounds plausible but unproven, and `experiment` if you already know the question and need the fastest test.
